Main Characters

Ted Lasso

An American football coach hired to manage AFC Richmond despite no soccer experience. Ted's unrelenting optimism and emotional intelligence help him connect with his players and transform the team.

Rebecca Welton

The owner of AFC Richmond who initially hired Ted hoping he would fail. She eventually becomes one of his strongest allies and friends.

Roy Kent

A veteran midfielder with a gruff exterior who later becomes part of the coaching staff. Roy's journey involves learning to be vulnerable and finding purpose after his playing career.

Coach Beard

Ted's long-time assistant coach who follows him to England. Beard is philosophical, eccentric, and fiercely loyal to Ted.

Keeley Jones

A model and public relations specialist who helps reshape AFC Richmond's image and eventually starts her own PR firm.

Nathan Shelley

The kit man who rises to become an assistant coach. His character undergoes significant development throughout the series.
